# Office and Productivity

> A curated list of free and open-source office and productivity software, including suites, project management, and note-taking tools.

## Word Processing and Office Suites
- **LibreOffice**: Full suite (Writer, Calc, Impress, Draw, Base, Math) 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [libreoffice.org](https://www.libreoffice.org/)
- **OnlyOffice**: Modern alternative with real-time collaboration 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [onlyoffice.com](https://www.onlyoffice.com/)
- **Calligra Suite**: Office suite for KDE environments 🪟 🐧 → [calligra.org](https://calligra.org/)

## Project Management
- **Taiga**: Agile tool (Scrum/Kanban) for teams 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [taiga.io](https://taiga.io/)
- **OpenProject**: Project management with task tracking, Gantt, and collaboration 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [openproject.org](https://www.openproject.org/)
- **Redmine**: Flexible project management with bug tracking 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [redmine.org](https://www.redmine.org/)

## Note-Taking
- **Joplin**: Note-taking with sync (Markdown, E2E encrypted) 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [joplinapp.org](https://joplinapp.org/)
- **Obsidian**: Markdown notes with knowledge graphs 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md/)
- **Logseq**: Outliner with Markdown support and local databases 🪟 🍎 🐧 → [logseq.com](https://logseq.com/)
